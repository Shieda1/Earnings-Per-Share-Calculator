What is the earnings per share?
The earnings per share (EPS) is a financial measurement that indicates the profitability of a company. It is a portion of the firm's profit that is allocated to every share of the stock.
How to calculate earnings per share?
To calculate earnings per share you should use the following formula:
earnings per share = (net income - preferred stock dividends) / average outstanding common shares
